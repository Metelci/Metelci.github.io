About

Turning ambitious study goals into daily wins.

I'm Metelci, a developer who loves pairing pragmatic engineering with behaviour-driven UX. Recent work spans an Android companion for 30-week English exam prep and a social web app that keeps study crews aligned.

Data privacy, accessibility, and measurable progress guide every iteration. Whether I am working in Kotlin, React, or Blazor, I focus on helping people show up consistently and celebrate real improvement.

  • Product mindset: Ship quickly, measure outcomes, and refine with feedback loops.
  • Security-first: Biometric auth, encrypted local storage, and careful defaults by design.
  • Community driven: Build features that make learning collaborative instead of lonely.

Road to YDS

Android app for 30-week YDS and YOKDIL preparation

A Kotlin and Jetpack Compose experience that guides learners with a full curriculum, smart reminders, and deep-dive analytics.

  • Phase-based study roadmap keeps progress visible across six months.
  • Biometric authentication, encrypted storage, and certificate pinning protect exam data offline.
  • Achievement system, streaks, and countdown widgets sustain daily commitment.
